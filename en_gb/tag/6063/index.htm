3104 aluminum

3104 aluminum alloy main alloying addition is manganese, it has high tensile strength ≥ 275Mpa, elongation up to 20%, excellent deep drawing and ductility. The most common applications are beverage cans and beer cans.

5005 Aluminium Sheet

5005 aluminium sheet and plate contains 0.8% magnesium, It has medium strength, good weldability, commonly available in flat rolled aluminum coil, sheet and plate from a wide range of producing mills.

5 bar aluminium checker plate

5 bar aluminium checker plate (tread plate) is a smooth sheet with a regular diamond pattern on one side. It is used for flooring, cladding, tool boxes, ramps or anything that requires an ultra-hard wearing property.

aluminium diamond plate

Aluminum diamond plate sometimes called aluminum tread plate, aluminum floor plate, or aluminum checker plate. the common size is 4x8', the thickness is 1/8, 3/16, 1/4, etc., it provides intensity, non-slip, and durability.

Thick aluminium foil

Thick Aluminium Foil is thicker than regular aluminium foil. Some thicker aluminium foils or household  extra heavy duty aluminium foils can be up to 0.94 mils thick.

5052 Aluminium Sheet Plate

5052 aluminium sheet  is alloyed with 2.5% magnesium and 0.25% chromium. 5052 aluminium sheet easily welded and highly corrosion-resistant.

Heavy duty aluminium foil

Heavy Duty Aluminium Foil is a thicker aluminium foil.Can be cut into 18 inch x 500ft and other sizes, silver or colored. offers extra protection against both high heat and freezing cold, making it perfect for cooking that calls for high-quality performanc.Find heavy duty aluminium foil supplier near you.

5X10 aluminium sheet and plate

5x10 aluminium sheet is available in lengths and widths of 5 feet and 10 feet. A variety of alloy grades of aluminium sheet can be cut to this size. Common thicknesses include 1/4', 1/8', 1/16', 0.125, 0.32, 0.40, etc.

4X8 aluminium sheet plate

The 4x8 aluminium sheet plate is used for truck and trailer skins, ships, buildings, can be painted in different colors, and the surface can be processed into tread plate, diamond plate. Factory price for sale.

6082 aluminium suppliers T6 temper

6082 aluminium alloy is an alloy in the wrought aluminium-magnesium-silicon family.It is one of the more popular alloys in 6000 series (alongside alloys 6005, 6061, and 6063). It is not used in casting, It cannot be work hardened.

6061 aluminium sheet

6061 aluminium sheet have 0.8%-1.2% magnesium. Lions aluminium can provide you 6061 aluminium sheet with reliable quality and prompt delivery. Common specifications are in stock. Special sizes can be customized according to clients requests.

5754 aluminium sheet plate

Alloy 5754 aluminium sheets & plates are magnesium-aluminium alloys. Typical uses include automotive body panels, shipbuilding, treadplate, vehicle bodies, rivets, fishing industry equipment.

What is the physical properties of aluminium?

Aluminium metal has an appearance ranging from silvery white to dull gray,Pure aluminium is quite soft and lacking in strength.The density of aluminium is 2.70 g/cm3, about 1/3 that of steel, much lower than other commonly encountered metals.

What is aluminium 6061 t6 properties?

6061 T6 aluminium properties is: Tensile Yield Strength:276 MPa,Elongation at Break:12%,Elongation at Break:17%,Modulus of Elasticity:68.9 GPa

How thick is 18 gauge aluminium?

The thickness of 18 gauge aluminium is 0.0403 inch, which is 1.024 mm in millimeters. Please refer to the 18 gauge aluminium weight conversion chart to calculate the weight.

How thick is 20 gauge aluminium?

The thickness of 20 gauge aluminium is 0.032 inch, which is 0.813 mm in millimeters. Please refer to the 20 gauge aluminium weight conversion chart to calculate the weight.
