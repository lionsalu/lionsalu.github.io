Aluminum sheet pre-roll coating process

The wood looks aluminum powder coating process can be divided into three steps:

  • Base Coat – Manufacturers apply a base coat of powder to the aluminum sheet. The powder is electrostatically charged to enhance adhesion.
  • Curing – The wood look aluminum sheet is placed in a curing oven. Typically they’re kept in the oven for about 10 minutes, at temperatures of around 200 degrees Celsius.
  • Cool Down – The wood look aluminum sheet is removed from the curing oven and allowed to cool down.
